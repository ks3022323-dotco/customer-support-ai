FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - ABC Technologies

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: Yes, you can change your plan anytime from Dashboard > Settings > Subscription.

Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?
A: Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes, available on iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

Q: How do I add more users?
A: Go to Dashboard > Team Management > Add User.

Q: What is the uptime SLA?
A: We guarantee 99.9% uptime for Professional and Enterprise plans.

Q: How do I export my data?
A: Go to Dashboard > Settings > Data Export and select your preferred format.

Q: Do you offer training?
A: Yes, free onboarding training is available for all plans. Enterprise customers get dedicated training sessions.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email: support@abctechnologies.com | Chat: Available in dashboard | Phone: 1-800-ABC-TECH (Enterprise only)
