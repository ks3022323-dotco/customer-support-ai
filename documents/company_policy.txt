COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT - ABC Technologies

REFUND POLICY:
- Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Annual subscriptions are eligible for pro-rated refunds.
- Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
- Refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a supervisor.

SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION:
- Customers can cancel at any time.
- Cancellation takes effect at end of billing cycle.
- All cancellation requests require supervisor approval.

ACCOUNT CLOSURE:
- Account closure requests require 48-hour processing time.
- All data is deleted after 30 days of account closure.
- Account closure requires supervisor approval.

COMPENSATION POLICY:
- Compensation may be offered for service outages exceeding 4 hours.
- Compensation requests are reviewed by the billing supervisor.

SUPPORT HOURS:
- Technical support available 24/7.
- Billing support: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
- Sales support: Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 8 PM.
